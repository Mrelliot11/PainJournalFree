Privacy Policy for Pain Tracker Free

Effective Date: April 6, 2025
Developer: Elliot Dubrow
Contact: eaushroom@gmail.com

Overview

Pain Tracker Free is a fully offline, local-only application. We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

Data Collection

We do not collect any data. Pain Tracker Free does not:

  • Connect to the internet
  • Transmit any information to external servers
  • Use analytics or crash reporting services
  • Display advertisements
  • Require an account or login

Data Storage

All data you enter (pain logs, settings, preferences) is stored exclusively on your device using your device's local storage. This data never leaves your device unless you choose to export it yourself.

Exports

If you use the CSV or PDF export feature, the resulting files are saved to your device's local storage. You are solely responsible for how you handle or share those files.

Permissions

Pain Tracker Free requests the following permissions:

  • Notifications — to deliver optional pain logging reminders you configure
  • Receive Boot Completed — to reschedule reminders after your device restarts

No permission is used to collect or transmit data.

Children's Privacy

Pain Tracker Free does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted with a new effective date.
